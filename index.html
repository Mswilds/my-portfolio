My Story

Hi, I’m Mindy Wilds!
Founder of Mindy Wilds Consulting!

I help PACE organizations strengthen interdisciplinary teams, improve care planning, and create systems that keep participants at the center of every decision.

I bring more than 30 years of nursing experience to my work, including over 14 years in the Program of All-Inclusive Care for the Elderly (PACE). My PACE journey began at Palmetto SeniorCare in Columbia, South Carolina, where I served as a Home Care Coordinator and discovered my passion for the model’s integrated, participant-centered approach. I quickly became a Center Director and then the Executive Director of that program. Since then, I’ve held leadership roles across California, including Executive Director of WelbeHealth Sequoia PACE, Regional Vice President of Operations, and Vice President of PACE Excellence. These experiences deepened my belief that great care depends on strong teams, clear processes, and confident facilitators. Today, I partner with PACE organizations to assess challenges, design practical solutions, and guide teams toward stronger collaboration and better outcomes. I think of my work as architecting for PACE—building the frameworks and habits that help teams do their best work for the people they serve.

Services

Interdisciplinary Team Alignment & Case Facilitation

For programs where care planning feels stuck or siloed

Includes:

  • Real-time or retrospective facilitation of complex participant cases
  • Coaching for IDT facilitators on guiding discussion, managing conflict, and ensuring participant-centered decisions
  • Deconstructing “stuck” cases to uncover where logic, structure, or culture needs a reset

PACE Program Architecture & Operations Advising

For organizations scaling or stabilizing PACE services

Includes:

  • Operational audits of clinical and IDT processes
  • Implementation plans to correct misalignment or dysfunction
  • Leadership coaching for Executive Directors or Clinical Leaders
  • Navigation of compliance requirements without losing the heart of care

Clinical Care Planning Calibration

For leadership teams who want to raise the floor and ceiling of participant care

Includes:

  • Development of care planning templates, triggers, and escalation logic
  • Training on how to center participant voice and values in IDT
  • Documentation coaching that aligns care with chart

Optional Add-ons

  • Virtual or in-person “IDT Tune-Up” sessions
  • 1:1 coaching for new facilitators or clinical leaders
  • Pre-CMS audit preparation focused on interdisciplinary process
